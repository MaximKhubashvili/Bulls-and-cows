Rules of the game :
Your task is to guess the hidden four-digit number , consisting of various numbers.
The number of bulls - this is the number of digits you have guessed , and by location and value.
The number of cows - is the number of digits you have guessed by value, but by location You could not guess.
The game stops when the number of bulls will be equal to four , in this case, you have won!
